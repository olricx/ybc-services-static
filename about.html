About Us

Putting Customers First

Since 1996 YBC Services has provided premium facilities management services through a combination of innovation, professionalism and industry expertise.

This was the vision of YBC’s founder and Managing Director, Yogen Chhetri, a former Brigade of Gurkha soldier who worked his way up in the industry to develop a company that is now at the cutting edge.

Today, through a range of integrated solutions we meet and exceed expectations of the varying needs of the businesses and organisations across the South of England.

YBC Mission Statement

Your Building Counts to us at YBC Services. We work in partnership with our stakeholders to deliver service excellence in a safe, caring and sustainable way.

Our core values of reliability, honesty and hard work underpin our commitment to providing bespoke high-quality and market leading services. We embrace new technology both in terms of continuous improvement and to minimise our environmental impact.

Eco and Our Future Together

Our internationally recognised accreditation of ISO 14001;2015 is at the heart of our company branding, exercising sustainable strategies for our future business growth, creating passionate employees with pride and commitment in their workplace.

The concept of reducing our environmental impact relies on suppliers with the same high level of credibility and ambitions as YBC Services are built on. We are working proactively and continuously within health and safety regulations, which impacts our business, our staff and carbon footprint.

In partnership with our customers, we have successfully introduced a number of “green” and sustainable initiatives across our business, using eco-friendly alternatives to conventional cleaning materials and methods, without affecting quality or service.

Our compliance with ecolabels is a minimum requirement for materials that also include our equipment, processes and methods that create tangible added value to your business.

As a caring employer who values their employees in the organisation, we aim to provide healthier and safer environments that will help improve efficiencies.

Our Green Initiatives

  • Chemical free cleaning using the power of electrically charged ozone water O3
  • UV sanitising wand
  • Never using bleach
  • Reach and wash systems
  • Auto-dosing systems
  • Reducing carbon emissions through monthly monitoring of fuel level consumptions of our fleet and regular servicing to ensure maximum efficiency.
  • Reduction in transport and storage
  • Recover, and recycle materials and packaging wherever possible with landfill disposal is the last resort
  • Non-functioning equipment is repaired as much as possible instead of disposed.
  • Aiming to transition to hybrid-based engine by 2030
  • Limiting water consumption
  • Purchasing locally

Benefits to our customers

  • Reduction in chemicals and cost
  • Improved quality and service
  • Right first-time attitude
  • Using correct dispensers/dilution ratios
  • Independent audits demonstrate our commitment to the environment and quality

Training

We make sure you meet all the needs that are required

It is essential that all our people possess the skills and competencies required to fulfil their role and meet the needs of our customers.

Training at YBC is comprehensive and the vast majority of it is undertaken internally. Our personnel are our greatest asset and as such are all trained to our Skill and Competency Standard. This creates a minimum skill standard ensuring that all personnel have the skills and knowledge required to complete all tasks to our accredited standard.

We offer staff the following training

  • Induction training to new staff
  • Development skills and understanding
  • Refresher training
  • Continuous development
  • Career development
  • Tool Box Talks
  • On-line training modules
  • Language skills
  • Management training
  • Annual refresher training agreed at annual appraisal when employee training matrices are updated

Communication is key

Training needs are identified through regular communication and checks carried out by the Supervisor. This is the best method to identify where improvements need to be made before they have an impact on the contract. Our Supervisors / Managers carry out refresher training when a need is identified for further improvement.

The culture of our company is to seek continuous improvement from all of our personnel and this is ingrained within our management and supervision training to direct and educate personnel to adopt this positive approach to their work. We know that some employees are not developed to fulfil their potential, but through training and opportunities for growth within the company staff are more motivated.
