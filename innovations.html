Innovations

Innovations

We ensure functionality, comfort, safety and efficiency of the built environment by integrating people, place, process and technology

Lotus Pro Aqueous Ozone

We are helping to achieve our sustainability targets by introducing the Lotus Pro Aqueous Ozone machine which can turn ordinary tap water, through a process of electrolysis with oxygen to create ozone water (O3), that has the power to clean every surface without any added chemicals.

By replacing chemicals with ozone water, cost savings of 40% on materials budgets can be achieved as no chemicals are used in the cleaning process with only a possible exception of using de-scaler products for toilet enamel surfaces and Selgiene Ultra anti-viral disinfectant.

In addition, to saving money, the introduction of ozone water helps us to achieve our sustainability targets by reducing the chemical usage and reducing carbon footprint as there is no need to transport chemicals. Thus, the effect our operation has on the environment is minimised.

i-Mop

The external aspect of your premises can have a big effect on your business. How your customers, clients, staff or students feel about the outside of your building can affect the way they behave when inside it. YBC can support you to enhance the first impressions and improve the overall environment of your facilities, making them truly welcoming and conducive to an effective working environment.

Lockable Janitor Trolley

Our day janitors are provided with i-mop janitor trollies specially designed with a docking station to charge the i-mop when it is not in use, ensuring that it can be used for an extended period of time. The trollies come complete with lockable cupboards for security reasons, concealed waste hopper to pick up litter and debris and colour coded containers.

Electrostatic Spray Gun

In a response to the COVID-19 pandemic outbreak, we have purchased a number of electrostatic spray guns as part of our revolutionary cleaning and disinfection system. These guns electrostatically charge the water infused with anti-viral disinfectant, which causes the fine aerosol generated by the gun to coat all sides of a surface adhering to hidden and shadowed areas, with no runs or drips on vertical surfaces, delivering rapid and effective disinfection to all surfaces and areas.

As the surfaces remain charged, they repel any bacteria or viruses for an extended period of time. Thus, we can disinfect a whole room with ease, effectively clearing organic matter (the source of outbreaks), killing virus and bacteria.

UV Wand

We use UV wands as a training tool to enable managers to demonstrate to their team areas that have been missed. They highlight areas of ‘invisible’ contamination and sterilise the areas they illuminate. The wand can kill up to 99.99% of germs in five minutes by holding it 3 cm above the surface you want to clean and allowing it to hover for two seconds.

The wand is safe to use, the UV rays do not cause skin irritation as the wand automatically turns off when turned over.

Kodak Telescopic Inspection Camera

Where there are high level areas requiring dusting that are difficult to see, we use a Kodak Telescopic Inspection Camera to view the dust. This gives us a better perspective on high-level areas without the need for scaffolding and it eliminates the risks that come with high-level cleaning.
